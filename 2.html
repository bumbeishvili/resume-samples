Marchetti
Brand Strategist · Milan / New York
Across fifteen years and two continents, Elena Marchetti has shaped brand narratives for cultural institutions, luxury houses, and technology companies that refuse to sound like everyone else. Her work sits at the intersection of strategy and storytelling — equal parts analytical rigour and editorial instinct. She has led engagements with Fondazione Prada, Apple Retail, and the Venice Biennale, building identities that resonate far beyond their launch window.
